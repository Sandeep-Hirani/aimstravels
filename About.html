About US

Founded in August of 2008 and based in Karachi, Pakistan , Aims is a trusted agency for people to list, discover, and book unique accommodations around the world. We provide services to people of all ethnicities, races and nationalities. Moreover we are trained to cater to special needs individuals as well providing them with an equally rewarding experience as any other.
Whether it’s an apartment for a night, a castle for a week, or a villa for a month, Aims connects people to unique travel experiences, at any price point, in more than 34,000 cities and 191 countries. And with world-class customer service, state-of-the-art technology and a growing number of satisfied customers Aims plans on fulfilling all your travelling desires.
